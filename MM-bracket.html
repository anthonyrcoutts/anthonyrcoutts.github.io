Extracted advanced statistical data from college basketball teams in previous March Madness tournaments. Ran a random forest predictor to predict what round teams would make it to on the data. Used feature importances to see what stats were important for winning in the tournament. Built a bracket in Excel with current teams. Created a score function for each team using the feature importances from the random forest as weights for the different stats with a randmized multiplier attatched to the function. Team with the higher score in each matchup advanced to the next round until a champion was crowned. Ran 500 simulations in Excel.

Skills Used:

Python SKLearn (Random Forests) R Excel Data Analysis Data Collection
