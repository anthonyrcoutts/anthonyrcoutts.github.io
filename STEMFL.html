Analyzed the success of the STEM Faculty Launch program held at WPI from 2015-2020. The program was designed for minorities looking to pursure a career in higher education in STEM fields. Analyzed application data to review where aplicants came from and designed an animated plot for it. Analyzed the job outcome those who attended the faculty launch vs those who applied but did not attend. Filled in missing job outcome data using open sources such as Linkedin and personal webpages.
