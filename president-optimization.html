Optimized the campaign schedule of presidential candidate Kamala Haris for the 2024 election in a team with two other students. Used the change on win percentage after visiting a state to measure effect of visiting a state. Extrapolated effect of visiting a state of univisited states by grouping state and averaging effects amoungst the group. Found the cost of visisting certain states and similairly extrapolated data for unvisited states. Optimized which states Kamala Harris should visit to boost her win percentage in states and gain the most electoral college votes.
